PLTW Chess Pieces

Fusion 360 • 3D Modeling • Manufacturing Prep

Designed as part of the PLTW Introduction to Engineering Design curriculum, this chess set showcases parametric modeling skills using Fusion 360. Each piece was modeled from scratch to demonstrate use of revolves, patterns, and lofts.

The set was dimensioned for consistent manufacturing and exported as STL files for 3D printing. Students also practiced tolerance planning for fit and visual alignment across the set.
