PLTW Chess Pieces

CAD • Design • PLTW IED

This was a fun modeling project for my high school PLTW IED class, where we got to research a designer of our choice. I chose Richard "Dick" Teague and designed chess pieces inspired by his work.

I was especially inspired by the AMC Pacer. We printed the pieces as a class, which led to a delightful set of slightly misshapen figurines. I was responsible for designing the knight and the pawn.
