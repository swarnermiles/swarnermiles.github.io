Anodizing Station

Aluminum Finishing • Custom Power Supply • Chemistry + Engineering

This anodizing station was built to safely and efficiently anodize aluminum components in a school lab environment. It includes a custom power control box, adjustable voltage settings, and insulated terminals.

The project integrates mechanical design, chemical process planning, and safe electrical handling — ideal for small batch parts like bike components or class projects. Anodized finishes were tested with dye immersion and UV stability.
