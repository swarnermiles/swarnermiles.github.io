Anodizing Station

Chemistry • Machining • Surface Engineering

This project was completed as part of my 10th grade Chemistry class, where I decided to create a 9" by 9" puzzle from 6061 aluminum. The pieces were waterjet-cut and then anodized to explore surface coating and material transformation.

Each piece was measured using micrometers to assess the thickness increase from anodizing. The process taught me how to handle hazardous chemicals safely and showed how much you can accomplish using everyday resources from the hardware store.
