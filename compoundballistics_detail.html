Compound Ballistics

PLTW • High School Engineering • Rubber Band Physics

This was a PLTW High school project in which we addressed the challenge of creating a "large scale yard game similar to the classic board game battleship". We were tasked with building a ping pong launching machine that would compete against other machines.

The catch was that they had to be powered with 2 rubber bands. We ultimately didn't have the most successful design, but I helped guide the mechanics of the launcher and did the mechanical advantage research.
